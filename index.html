About

A Coming Together of the Citizens of Wakanda and Lovers of Black Panther

February 16, 2018 | Regal Thornton Place Stadium 14

The release of Marvel’s Black Panther has been long-awaited by the Black community at large. Therefore, to celebrate this occasion properly, we’ll be taking over the Regal Thornton Pl theater in Northern Seattle with the “Wakanda Homecoming” to view Black Panther together! There will be music, drinks, soul food, and friends all around.

In addition to the grand occasion that is Black Panther, we will also celebrate the grander occasion that is our hostess’ birthday! Patrice Bell wants to share this event with you, and will provide cake and champagne. We only ask that you join in a chorus of Stevie Wonder’s “Happy Birthday" song.

We can't wait to see you, and look forward to the this joyfully black occasion.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is date of this event?
This event will take place Friday, February 16th 2018.
Are there ID or minimum age requirements to enter the event?
No, all are welcome. However the movie rating is PG-13 so please plan accordingly. PLEASE use parental discretion as some scenes may not be suitable for younger viewers. There will be a cash bar on site that will require ID for service.
What are my parking options?
If you purchase the “Parking” ticket add-on, a parking space will be reserved for you in the parking garage directly below the theater. Your comp ticket will be distributed upon your arrival and check-in.
How can I contact the organizer with any questions?
Please contact us at Wakanda.Info@gmail.com or look to the Facebook event page for updates and latest information.
What's the refund policy?
Once tickets are purchased, they are NON-REFUNDABLE.
